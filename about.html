     About Our COMMUNITY FRIDGE

    Around 800 million people suffer from hunger globally, Chronic hunger creates a vicious cycle of malnutrition, stunted growth, and childhood death before the age of five. India ranks only 97 out of 118 developing countries by the Global Hunger Index. According to the United Nations Development Programme, up to 40% of the food produced in India is wasted.About 21 million tonnes of wheat are wasted in India and 50% of all food across the world meets the same fate and never reaches the needy.

    Nutrior will be a bridge between the need and the waste of food. Nutrior, is a chain of a Community Fridges. It is a new way to tackle food insecurity. Community fridge is type of mutual aid project which enables food to be shared within a community.
